About Me

Hyoseong Shin

I’m a Korea-based video designer with hands-on experience in motion graphics, 3D animation, and compositing. Having worked on high-impact campaigns for clients such as Tylenol, McCain, AmorePacific, and the Ministry of National Defense, I specialize in storytelling that combines precision with cinematic emotion.

I bring cross-functional experience across commercial, government, and branding content, handling everything from art direction to final compositing. My goal is to create work that resonates visually and emotionally, blending bold creativity with technical finesse.

Currently, I’m preparing to join a leading game studio like Pearl Abyss, bringing my real-time visual storytelling skills into immersive, interactive environments.
